1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the AppleVis iOS app.

This app is designed specifically for:
- VoiceOver users
- Braille users
- Low vision users

With the app, you can:
- Read and participate in forums
- Listen to podcasts
- Discover apps
- Read guides and articles

---

2. ACCESSIBILITY OVERVIEW

The app respects all iOS accessibility settings:

- VoiceOver
- Dynamic Type (text size)
- Dark Mode
- Reduce Motion
- Increased Contrast

The app does NOT override these settings.

---

3. GETTING STARTED

When you open the app:

- You land on the Home tab
- You can browse without signing in
- Signing in allows posting, replying, saving, and syncing

Navigation is done through tabs at the bottom:
Home, Community, Podcasts, Apps, Explore, Profile

---

4. UNDERSTANDING NAVIGATION

Each screen is structured:

- Screen title
- Sections (Headings)
- Lists of items

VoiceOver users can:
- Swipe right/left to move
- Use rotor to jump by headings or actions

---

5. HOME TAB (DETAILED)

The Home tab shows what matters most.

Sections:

Since Last Visit:
- Forums: number of new posts/replies
- Podcasts: number of new episodes
- Apps: number of updates

Example:
“Forums — 5 new”

Double tap to open Community filtered to unread.

Continue:
- Resume threads (opens at first unread)
- Resume podcasts
- Resume browsing apps or guides

Forum Activity:
- Active discussions
- Threads with new replies

Podcasts:
- Continue listening
- New episodes

Apps:
- Updated apps

16. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Do I need an account?
A: No, but it is required to post or reply.

Q: How do I reply?
A: Open a thread and use the Reply action.

Q: How do I find new content?
A: Use the Home tab under “Since Last Visit.”

Q: How do badges work?
A: The number shows how many new items you have.

Q: Can I listen offline?
A: Yes, download podcasts.

Q: How do I save items?
A: Use Bookmark actions.

Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Yes, for previously viewed content.

Q: How do I change notification sound?
A: Profile → Sound & Haptics.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Profile → Help & Support.

Q: What is “Jump to First Unread”?
A: It takes you to new comments in a thread.
