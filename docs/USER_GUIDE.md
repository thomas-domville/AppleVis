# AppleVis App User Guide — Draft

## Tabs

AppleVis has five main areas: Home, Forums, Podcasts, Apps, and Resources.

## Home

Home shows what changed since your last visit, saved items, continue listening, and resume where you left off.

## Forums

Use the filter control to choose Recent, New, Unread, Since Last Visit, Following, or Saved.

Recent shows active topics sorted by latest activity. New shows newly created topics. Unread shows topics with replies after your last-read point. Since Last Visit shows changes since you last opened AppleVis.

## Podcasts

The podcast player supports playback speed, skip back, skip forward, chapters, queue, downloads, saved episodes, transcripts when available, sleep timer, voice enhancement, and background playback.

## Apps

Browse app listings, reviews, updates, saved apps, and followed apps.

## Resources

Find guides, tutorials, how-to articles, accessibility resources, developer resources, and getting-started content.

## Saved Items

Saved items are available from Home and inside each tab. Save a topic, podcast, app, or resource to find it again later.

## Settings

Settings includes Account & Profile, Notifications, Appearance, Accessibility, Podcasts, Sounds & Haptics, Saved & Sync, Siri & Shortcuts, Help & Support, and About AppleVis.

## Contact AppleVis

Use Settings > Help & Support > Contact AppleVis to report a bug, send feedback, suggest a feature, report an accessibility issue, or ask for account help. Emails should be addressed to support@applevis.com.
